Suryanagari - E-Commerce Logistics and Customer Experience

Problem Space

The project addresses the optimization of e-commerce logistics and the enhancement of customer experience within the context of a smart city. As urban environments evolve, especially in smart cities powered by IoT, big data, and advanced algorithms, managing e-commerce logistics efficiently has become essential.

This problem space emerged due to the growing complexity of e-commerce logistics, where multiple factors such as product delivery, inventory management, pricing strategies, and customer segmentation need to be streamlined. With the rise of smart cities, IoT devices and real-time data analytics provide the potential to optimize these aspects, offering a more seamless experience for both businesses and customers.

Findings from White Paper

  • Automation and Optimization: Advanced algorithms like Dijkstra’s and Bellman-Ford optimize delivery routes.
  • Real-Time Data Usage: Smart city infrastructure generates data that can be leveraged for dynamic pricing and customer behavior analysis.
  • Customer-Centric Strategies: Personalization in marketing is crucial for customer retention and engagement.
  • Fraud Detection: Algorithms like Kruskal’s and Prim’s help identify potential fraudulent activities in online transactions.
  • Smart Infrastructure: IoT devices and intelligent transportation systems enhance logistics and improve customer satisfaction.

Problem Definition

The goal of this project is to enhance the efficiency of e-commerce logistics and improve the customer experience in a smart city. The project focuses on:

  • Optimizing Delivery Operations: Enhancing delivery route optimization for faster and cost-effective delivery.
  • Enhancing Customer Experience: Using dynamic pricing, personalized marketing, and product recommendations.
  • Inventory Management: Predicting demand to ensure optimal stock levels.
  • Fraud Prevention: Identifying and preventing fraudulent transactions using algorithms.
